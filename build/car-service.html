Car Service Application

Full-Stack Developer / Project Manager

Challenge

We inherited an old ground-transportation application that connected corporate buyers and travel agencies with car service providers. This division grew over the years but as new competitors entered the market—Uber, Lyft, etc.—its strategy became obsolete. In early 2015, we needed a new vision to continue competing in the space.

We decided to turn the current car service business into a real 2.0 version in which we would: migrate the product completely to the cloud; develop mobile apps for both ends, chauffeurs and consumers; leverage real-time geolocation technologies; rebrand product and refresh the User Interface (UI); and integrate the service with our Travel app, B2B solution for corporate travel.

My team and I worked on two sets of marketing and engineering challenges: Marketing and Rebranding strategy, product Web UI and Sign-Up process as well.

Roadmap

This ambitious project was divided into two phases. My team and I worked on phase one, which consisted of marketing and rebranding the new product offerings, refreshing the existing product UIs and automating the sign-up and onboarding process for greater scale.

  • Marketing goals:
    • Define go-to-market strategy and demand generation campaigns to increase sign-ups.
    • Brand and establish new product offerings and decommission obsolete ones.
  • Technical goals:
    • Modernize and refresh UI: API-driven architecture and code refactoring by levaraging new technologies and frameworks.
    • Redefine and develop new sign-up and onboarding process: seamless and predictable user flow, push data to multiple end-points (product, marketing/sales tools, analytics) for enhanced customer insights, etc.

Concept and Design

  • Make it easy: intuitive and usable.
  • Be consistent for both sets of customers: direct and white-label (branding, service standards, quality, privacy, permission, etc.).
  • Deliver on the customer promise.
  • Engage current and prospective users with intuitive and usable user-interface.

Implementation

  • Marketing challenges:
    • Leverage existing communication channels such as newsletters, webinars, email, nurture campaigns to drive awareness and create leads.
    • Execute an integrated marketing campaign that utilizes thought leadership and research-based content across multiple platforms: paid and organic search (SEO), blogs and social media.
    • Use targeted digital and print advertising across publications consumed by our target audience such as the travel-trade press.
  • Technical challanges:
    • Migrate business logic to RESTful API and consume it across multiple sites.
    • Refactor and/or integrate legacy stack (ASP, Client-Server Web architecture, XHTML, etc.).
    • Make site responsive and accessible by multiple devices and users.
    • Clean and simplify UI while maintaining resemblancce to previous design.

Success Factors

  • 10x increase in sign-ups.
  • Automated sign-up process, resulting in cost reduction and increased efficiency.
  • Improved and simplified overall UX.
  • Rebranded product, removing any ambiguity and communicating a consistent message.
  • Blurred the line between product and marketing sites through seamless sign-up/sign-in user-flows: discover, educate, convert and retain.
